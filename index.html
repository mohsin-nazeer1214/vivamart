Viva Mart Franchise Business Model

Vivi Mart is an online product delivery service that can be accessed via the internet. For the success of a delivery platform, the company needs both customers and manufacturers/suppliers to help it complete the supply chain.
Vivi Mart also functions as a standard e-commerce site, where a customer places an online order through the app or website and the goods are delivered within a specified time frame
Vivi Mart is a grocery shop that uses an online-to-offline (O2O) business model, in which consumers may search for products and place orders online before completing their purchases offline at the local Vivi Mart in their area. GVS Food & Retail Pvt Ltd already delivers Vivi Mart orders, but the company is developing a hybrid strategy that blends its retail locations with local Vivi Mart franchises to fulfill orders more quickly.
